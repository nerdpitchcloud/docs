# Introduction
Welcome to Nerdpitch - your platform for building, deploying, and scaling applications with ease.
## What is Nerdpitch?
Nerdpitch is a comprehensive cloud platform designed for developers and teams who want to deploy and manage their applications without the complexity of traditional cloud providers.
We handle the infrastructure so you can focus on building great software.
## Key Features
- **Custom Software Development** - We build tailored solutions for your specific needs
- **Container Orchestration** - Docker Swarm and Kubernetes support out of the box
- **Auto-scaling** - Scale from zero to millions of requests automatically
- **Pay-as-you-go** - Only pay for what you actually use
## Who is Nerdpitch for?
### Developers
Build and deploy your applications with a simple workflow. Push your code, we handle the rest.
### Teams
Collaborate with your team using projects, shared resources, and granular permissions.
### Enterprises
Run mission-critical workloads with enterprise-grade security and compliance features.
